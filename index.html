Welcome to LiveSpace


LiveSpace is a national full-service audio, video, and lighting integrator, and a live event production, staging and rental company based in Grand Rapids, MI.

We specialize in intelligent system design, clean installation, seamless integration, and exceptional live event production. From conception to completion, our goal is to create dynamic sensory experiences while exceeding expectations.

The markets and spaces in which we thrive include: house of worship, hospitality, retail, food and beverage, performance-centric, corporate conventions, concerts, festivals and entertainment venues.
